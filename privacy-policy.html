Privacy Policy for ZeppelinGames

Last updated Mars 20, 2025

At ZeppelinGames, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our users. This privacy policy applies to all the mobile games developed by ZeppelinGames. It explains the types of data we collect, how we use it, and the third-party services we work with.


1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data for analysis and performance purposes:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our games, such as gameplay activity, session length, and in-game actions.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including device type, operating system version, and device identifiers.
  • Analytics Data: Data collected through various third-party services to understand player behavior and improve the gameplay experience.

2. Third-Party Services

Depending on the game, we may use one or more of the following third-party services to enhance functionality, display ads, and analyze performance:


  • * GameAnalytics: Used to collect and analyze usage and performance data.
  • * Firebase: Used for analytics, crash reporting, and other backend services.
  • * Unity Ads: Used for displaying advertisements in our games.
  • * AdMob: Used for displaying advertisements in our games.

These services may collect personal information such as device identifiers and usage data. We do not have direct access to the data collected by these services, and they are responsible for their own data practices. For more information, please refer to their privacy policies:


  • GameAnalytics Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Privacy Policy

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We do not use cookies or similar technologies in our games. However, third-party services like Unity Ads, AdMob, Firebase, and GameAnalytics may use cookies or tracking technologies for their own purposes.
