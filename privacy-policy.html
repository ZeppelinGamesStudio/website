ZeppelinGames Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 29, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

ZeppelinGames is committed to protecting your privacy in all our mobile games and applications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you download and play our mobile games.

Information We Collect in Our Apps

Device Information:

We collect device type, operating system version, device model, and app version to ensure smooth gameplay.

Game Progress Data:

Your progress, scores, and settings are saved locally and may sync across your devices to enhance your experience.

Usage Analytics:

We collect anonymous data on how players interact with our games to improve gameplay and fix issues. No personal information is collected.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our mobile games are designed for children and fully comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not collect any personal information from children under 13.

Some games may offer optional sharing features that allow players to share non-personal achievements or progress. Sharing is always controlled by the user, and no personal information is collected or shared.

Our games do not include chat features, social media integration, or any way for children to communicate with strangers.

If you have any concerns about your child's privacy, please contact us and we will promptly address them.

App Permissions

Our apps may request the following permissions:

  • Storage: To save your game progress and settings
  • Network Access: To sync game data
  • Notifications: To send optional reminders related to gameplay

We only request permissions necessary for game functionality and never access unnecessary device features.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our mobile games
  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Develop new games and features
  • Ensure app security and prevent cheating

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to help improve our apps, including:

  • Firebase: For analytics, crash reporting, and cloud save. Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads: For in-game advertising. Privacy Policy
  • AdMob: For serving ads. Privacy Policy

These services have their own privacy policies. We ensure all are COPPA-compliant for children's apps.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Game data is encrypted during transmission and stored securely. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

We do not collect or store personal information. Your game progress is saved locally on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.

Analytics data collected is anonymous and cannot be linked to you personally, so it is not available for individual access requests.

If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us using the information below.
